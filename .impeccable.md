## Design Context

### Users
French expat/travel content creator audience — people who follow François on Instagram, curious about his life abroad, and aspiring creators who want to build their own website. They discover this site through social media and arrive with low attention spans but high curiosity.

### Brand Personality
Warm, adventurous, human. François is a French traveler who codes on Sundays, makes friends everywhere, and believes in sharing. The site should feel like hanging out with a charismatic friend — not a cold tech portfolio.

### Aesthetic Direction
Cinematic warmth with magical sparkle moments. Think golden-hour travel photography meets cozy French joie de vivre. The "shiny" moments use starfield sparkles — twinkling light particles, glints, and celebratory warmth — not chrome or sci-fi. Primary palette: amber/warm stone tones. Never cold, never corporate.

Anti-references: cold dark-mode dev portfolios, purple/cyan gradients, glassmorphism-heavy SaaS.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth first** — every surface should feel inviting, not intimidating
2. **Sparkle with purpose** — magical moments (hero, CTAs) use light particles/glints to delight without distracting
3. **Human over polished** — imperfect, personal, storytelling beats slick and generic
4. **Convert gently** — the e-book is the hero product; surface it early but never pushy
5. **Motion that breathes** — animations should feel organic, like sunlight through trees, not mechanical
